Garlic & Butter Fried Chicken

An all time favorite dish that loved by all ages!

Ingredients

  • 600g of Chicken
  • 1 sachet of Magic Sarap
  • 1/4 tsp of Ground pepper
  • 1/2 cup of All purpose flour
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • 6 cloves of Garlic

Steps:

  1. Season chicken wings with MAGGI MAGIC SARAP® and pepper.
  2. Coat well with flour. Pan fry in preheated oil until golden brown. Set aside to drain excess oil.
  3. Sauté garlic in butter. Add fried chicken and coat with the garlic and butter. Transfer on a serving plate and serve.
